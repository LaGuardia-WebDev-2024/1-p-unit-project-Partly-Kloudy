Why Do I Like The Color Green?

Green is such a rich color, literally! Dollar bills are green. Wealth is green. Greed is green. But that is not why I like green.

Green Representation

Green is represents many different things related to greed and selfishness, but green also represents:

  • Growth
  • Health
  • Nature
  • Peace
  • Hope

Green Plants, Fruits, And Vegetables

Examples of green plants, fruits and vegetables are:

  • Brussel Sprouts
  • Avacado
  • Lime
  • Cilantro
  • Mint
