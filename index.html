Why Do I Like The Color Green?💚

What a beautiful, verdant landscape above! Whenever I pass by this kind of scenery, I can't take my eyes off!
How beautiful and vibrant green is, green is such a rich color, literally! Dollar bills are green. Wealth is green. Greed is green. Green can be used to represent a character's envy or selfishness. But these representations are not why I like green.

Green Representation

Green represents many different things related to greed and money, but green also represents:

  • Growth
  • Health
  • Nature
  • Peace
  • Hope

These green representation examples above is why I like green.
